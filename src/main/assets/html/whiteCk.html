"White Chicks" is a 2004 American comedy film directed by Keenen Ivory Wayans. The movie stars Shawn Wayans and Marlon Wayans as two African American FBI agents who go undercover as white women to solve a kidnapping plot. Disguised as wealthy socialites, they navigate a series of comedic misadventures while trying to maintain their cover. The film explores themes of race and identity through its outrageous and humorous scenarios. Despite mixed reviews from critics, "White Chicks" gained a significant cult following for its slapstick humor and memorable performances.
